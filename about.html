Company

The people building Tixible.

Tixible is a small, deliberate team building a technology company meant to last for decades — starting with the people, not just the products.

Tixible started from a simple frustration: every team we'd ever worked with was stitching together a dozen tools from a dozen vendors, each with its own design language, its own support queue, and its own way of billing. None of it felt like it belonged to a single, coherent product family.

So in 2025, Tixible was founded on a different premise: build the company as one system from the start. Tixible Cloud — launching August 1, 2026 — is the first proof of that idea, and the foundation every future product will stand on.

We're early. The team is small on purpose. But the ambition is not — Tixible is being built to grow into a lasting, multi-product technology company, one deliberate decision at a time.

Mission
Build software that businesses, developers, and teams can depend on for the long term.
Vision
Become the platform behind a new generation of connected digital tools.
Principles
